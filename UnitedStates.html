The United States


California (San Fransico):

Breakfast - Just For You Cafe is a beloved neighborhood brunch spot that perfectly captured the cozy, old-school charm of San Francisco’s Dogpatch area. Known for its warm, friendly service and hearty comfort food, it earned a loyal following thanks to standout dishes like fluffy beignets, rich Hangtown fry, and classic American breakfast plates. The atmosphere is casual and welcoming, making it a go-to place for everything from relaxed weekend brunches to satisfying hangover cures. While simple in concept, the quality ingredients and generous portions made it a memorable dining experience that locals returned to again and again.

Lunch - The Little Chihuahua is a vibrant, go-to spot for fresh and flavorful Mexican food in San Francisco. Known for its laid-back atmosphere and colorful vibe, it serves up delicious tacos, burritos, and aguas frescas made with high-quality ingredients. The menu offers something for everyone, including great vegetarian options, and the bold, well-balanced flavors keep people coming back. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or a casual meal with friends, it’s a reliable choice for satisfying, feel-good food in the city.

Dinner - Spruce offers an elevated dining experience that blends refined elegance with warm hospitality. Known for its Michelin-starred cuisine, the restaurant delivers beautifully presented dishes inspired by seasonal ingredients and classic American flavors with a modern twist. The sophisticated yet inviting atmosphere, paired with an exceptional wine list and attentive service, makes it an ideal destination for special occasions or a memorable night out. Every detail—from the ambiance to the artistry of each plate—reflects a commitment to quality and a truly exceptional dining experience.


Missouri (St. Louis):

Breakfast - Messenger Coffee Co. + Ibis Bakery is a standout coffee destination that blends expertly crafted drinks with a vibrant, multi-level space perfect for working or relaxing. The atmosphere is bright and modern, with plenty of seating options and even a rooftop area that makes it feel more like an experience than just a quick coffee stop. Their coffee and house-made pastries are consistently praised for quality and flavor, making it a go-to spot for both locals and visitors. While it can get busy and a bit pricey at times, the overall vibe, friendly service, and attention to craft make it well worth the visit.

Lunch - Katie’s Pizza & Pasta Osteria delivers a fresh, elevated take on classic Italian dishes, with standout pizzas that feature high-quality ingredients and creative flavor combinations. The atmosphere strikes a great balance between upscale and relaxed, making it just as good for a casual night out as it is for a special occasion. Many guests rave about the crisp, flavorful crust and unique menu items, along with attentive, friendly service that enhances the experience. While prices can be on the higher side, most agree the quality and overall vibe make it well worth it.

Dinner - Salt + Smoke is a go-to spot for bold, flavorful barbecue, serving up everything from tender brisket to juicy pulled pork with a rich, smoky depth that keeps people coming back. The laid-back, modern atmosphere makes it an easy choice for anything from casual dinners to group hangouts, while sides like mac and cheese and duck-fat fries often steal the spotlight. Reviews consistently highlight the quality and variety of meats, along with creative touches like unique sauces and standout appetizers. While it can be a bit pricey and occasionally busy, the overall experience delivers satisfying comfort food with a polished, contemporary twist.


New York:

Breakfast - Nectar Café is a charming, health-focused spot known for its fresh juices, smoothies, and wholesome, organic menu options. The laid-back, cozy atmosphere makes it a great place to grab a quick bite or settle in for a relaxed, feel-good meal. Guests often highlight the vibrant flavors, quality ingredients, and friendly service that keep the experience consistently enjoyable. While it can be a bit on the pricier side for casual fare, the freshness and overall vibe make it a favorite for those looking to eat well without sacrificing taste.

Lunch - Amber is a cozy, under-the-radar sushi spot that delivers consistently fresh, high-quality rolls in a relaxed and welcoming setting. Amber stands out for its balance of classic sushi staples and creative specialty rolls, all made with attention to detail and flavor. The atmosphere is intimate and low-key, making it a great choice for anything from a casual dinner to a laid-back date night. While it’s not the flashiest sushi spot in NYC, the friendly service and solid value make it a reliable neighborhood favorite.

Dinner - Orsay is a classic Upper East Side gem that captures the charm of a Parisian brasserie with its elegant, Art Nouveau–inspired design and warm, inviting atmosphere. The menu leans into traditional French cuisine—think steak frites, duck confit, and rich appetizers—all executed with consistency and attention to detail. Service is polished and attentive, adding to the restaurant’s refined yet comfortable vibe that works equally well for date nights or leisurely dinners. While it sits on the pricier side, the combination of ambiance, quality dishes, and a strong wine list makes it a standout dining experience.
